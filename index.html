Who am I

HELLO.

I'm Vaishnavi, a UI designer with a strong foundation in frontend logic. I bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality—creating digital experiences that are intuitive, pixel-perfect, and built for seamless developer handoff.

I specialize in UI Design and Responsive Systems. My background in development means I design with implementation in mind—I deliver layouts that are technically sound and ready for production, ensuring there are no 'impossible' specs or design-dev gaps."

2024 — Started Learning
Discovered UI/UX design. Dived into Figma, design principles, and UX fundamentals.
2025 — Portfolio Building
Built Note Making App, E-commerce UI, and Dashboard projects from scratch.
2025 — Design for Implementation
Leveraging my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and React to create high-fidelity Figma designs that are perfectly aligned with modern development frameworks. I focus on delivering end-to-end design solutions that are easy for engineering teams to translate into code.
